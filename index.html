La
Malbaie

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

- Overview -

When ships docked here, the tide went out and they were beached. That’s why it was called La Malbaie; the bad bay.

The fjord Saguenay opens into the St. Lawrence River, which eventually opens into the Atlantic Ocean.

- Location -

La Malbaie is a small town in the Charlevoix municipality, an hour from the opening of the fjord Saguenay.

- Highlights -

- Hotels -

  1. La Manoir Richelieu
  2. This famous 5-star Fairmont hotel overlooks the St. Lawrence River
  3. La Villa Julie
  4. The Villa Julie is a private house available on AirBNB, where I stayed during my trip
  5. L'auberge Des 3 Canards
  6. L'auberge is a smaller hotel which overlooks the river. It originally got is name from the mispronounciation of the 'Three Docs' to the 'Three Ducks'

- Restaurants -

  1. Restaurant Allegro
  2. Cafe Chez-Nous
  3. Restaurant L'Orchidee

- Recommendation-

As somebody who thoroughly loves Quebec, I highly recommend this destination. The locals speak native French, though English is still understood. The views are excellent, the drive to Quebec City is only an hour, and an hour in the opposite direction is the fjord of Saguenay. Overall, service was great, food was excellent, and the experience was highly enjoyable. I would definitely go here again.
